Module Kat__Ast.Nary

N-ary expressions (modulo associativity).

type ('act, 'test) exp =
| Assert of 'test bexp
| Action of 'act
| Union of ('act, 'test) exp list
| Seq of ('act, 'test) exp list
| Star of ('act, 'test) exp

Seq [] and Union [] are equivalent to Assert True and Assert False, respectively.
